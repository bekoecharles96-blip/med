Deadly Diseases

These diseases can be fatal if not treated. Awareness is key.

Coronavirus (COVID-19)

A contagious respiratory disease spread through droplets and contact.

Cancer

Abnormal cell growth that destroys body tissue. Early detection improves survival.

Heart Attack

A serious condition where blood flow to the heart is blocked, damaging heart muscle.
